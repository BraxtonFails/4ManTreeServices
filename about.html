About Us

Hey, we're Caleb and Maddox — cousins and co-founders of 4Man Tree Services. We're both proud to call Dayton, Ohio our hometown.

We started this business in June 2024 with a simple goal: to provide high-quality, reliable tree care with a level of dedication you don't always see. Whether it's tree removal, trimming, stump grinding, or emergency cleanup, we treat every job like it's in our own backyard.

As a young crew with big ambition, we bring fresh energy, modern tools, and a strong work ethic to every project. We take pride in helping our neighbors keep their properties safe, clean, and looking great.

Thanks for checking us out — we'd love the chance to earn your trust and your business.

— Caleb & Maddox
